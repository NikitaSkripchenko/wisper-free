How it helps

Less typing after the call.

01

Wisper stays small and close by, so you can keep attention on the person speaking instead of the note-taking.

02

Leave with a readable transcript.

Turn the spoken parts into text you can copy into a recap, a document, a task list, or wherever the work goes next.

03

Finished transcripts stay in history, so the quote, decision, or follow-up is not buried in a pile of audio files.

Quiet software for noisy days.

Nothing joins your call. Wisper records from your Mac, so your notes do not announce themselves in every meeting.

Designed to disappear. A small floating control lets you pause, stop, or discard without pulling attention away from the person speaking.
